Our promise

“Honesty is our only policy — and we prove it with every repair.”

A wreck isn't just about a car. It's about the safety of you and your family. So we consult with you before we start, negotiate with the insurance company for the quality parts and service you deserve, and no vehicle leaves this shop until our specialists — and you — sign off on it.

What we repair

Everything after the accident, under one roof.

From a parking-lot scuff to a frame pushed in on the interstate, it's all done in-house in Gonzales — and we'll help you line up a rental car while we work.

  1. 01

    Collision Repair

    Light to severe. We rebuild crumpled panels, bumpers, and the safety systems behind them back to manufacturer spec — not just what shows on the surface.

  2. 02

    Auto Body & Paint

    Computerized color matching and a controlled refinish process for a factory-quality finish that blends seamlessly into the panels around it.

  3. 03

    Frame & Structural

    Measured frame and unibody straightening so your vehicle tracks true down the road and protects you the way it was engineered to in the next impact.

  4. 04

    Paintless Dent Repair

    For dings, hail, and door dents where the paint is intact, we massage the metal back out — no filler, no repaint, less cost.

  5. 05

    Auto Glass

    Chipped or shattered windshield and window glass replaced so you can see the road clearly and safely again.

  6. 06

    Insurance & Rental Help

    We work with every insurance company, handle the adjuster back-and-forth, and — if you've been in an accident — help you line up a rental car so you're never stranded.

How it works

From wrecked to back on the road.

No mystery, no runaround. Here's exactly what happens when you bring your vehicle to Guillory's.

  1. 1

    Free written estimate

    Bring the car by — or send us photos — and our specialists assess the damage and give you an honest, fair, written estimate. No pressure to repair.

  2. 2

    We handle the insurance

    We work directly with your insurance company and negotiate for the quality parts and proper repair you deserve — and help set you up with a rental if you need one.

  3. 3

    The repair — and you stay in the loop

    Body, paint, and frame work done in-house by people who've done it for decades. We keep you updated so you're never wondering where things stand.

  4. 4

    Two sign-offs, then keys

    Our specialists approve the repair first. Then we hand it to you for final approval — and back it with a lifetime warranty for as long as you own the vehicle.
